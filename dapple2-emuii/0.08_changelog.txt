Changes from 0.07 to 0.08
-------------------------
  * The patch for 0.08 applies over 0.07.  Your results on 0.07a may vary.
    Source is still lint-free.
  * The secret's out: experimental support for the Apple //c has been added.
  * Additional support for 65SC02 and original 6502 CPUs.
  * Emulation for screen refreshes corresponding to 0.5 or 2 MHz CPU speed.
  * Megadisk drive light.
  * If 640x400 or 640x480 can't be activated, try 800x600 or 1024x768.
  * Full or partial scanlines, if that's your cup of tea.
  * Dual screen mode (like ApplePC).
